Name, Section, and Batch?

Jheliane Alyna R. Labrador from 9-Arayat, batch 2026.

When did I get into Vocaloid?

I got into Vocaloid around third grade (6 years ago) when I first got my smartphone and internet access.

How did I get into Vocaloid?

It all started when I came across this video of Luka singing dreamin' chuchu. I immediately fell in love with Vocaloid after it. I started exploring the different producers, parts of the community, and vocaloids afterwards. It was like falling into a rabbit hole. I never truly got out of this rabbit hole as the first thing I did after getting my first laptop was to install UTAU and try to tune a voicebank myself. Unfortunately, I knew very little of how to tune that time and never reattempted after giving up on it the first time.

Favorite Vocaloid (+voicebank) and producer?

Even after exploring many vocaloids, Luka has always been my favorite due to the low tones of all her voicebanks. Despite this, my favorite voicebank is actually Miku Dark VX4. I absolutely love how Miku sounds in this voicebank. Finally, my favorite producer was actually mentioned in the recommended page who is Kasamura Toota. I'm a sucker for ballads, which is why I end up listening to all of her songs over and over again. *Luna and Niki are close second favorites though!
