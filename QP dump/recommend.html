flower (フラワ) is an official Vocaloid released by YAMAHA corporation but is not part of the 6 cryptonloids. As a vocaloid, flower's range is very flexible and songs using her for vocals tend to expand the limits of what sounds vocaloids can produce.
Video: Venom by Kairiki Bear (vb: flower)

Kasane Teto (重音テト) is an UTAU(loid). Rather than being available for use in the official vocaloid editing system, Teto is available in UTAU, a free synth editor. Despite being created as a joke, Teto is one of the voice synths that are the easiest to use and provide realistic vocals. Due to her amazing vocal quality, Teto is usually mistaken for an official vocaloid.
Video: Rolling Girl (ballad arrangement) by Teto Chan (vb: Kasane Teto)

Kasamura Tota (傘村トータ) is a Vocaloid Producer that has a distinct tuning style. Her choice in melodies and tunes are accompanied by soulful lyrics that have a melancholic atmosphere.
Video: Give Me the Courage to Give Up the Happiness I Wanted So Much by Kasamura Tota (vb: Hatsune Miku)

niki is a Vocaloid Producer that is most known for using Lily in his songs, thus gaining him the "Legendary Lily Master" title given by the Japanese community of Vocaloid. After some time, he learned to use other Vocaloids other than lily. His songs are full of emotion and are displayed through the heavy rock themes in his songs.
Video: Hearts by niki (vb: Lily)

Sasanomaly, also known as nekobolo, is a Vocaloid Producer similar to Kasamura Tota who specializes in slow, melancholic songs. Unlike Kasamura who has focuses on Vocaloid, Sasanomaly is a cover artist (Utaite) who also sings the songs he writes. Through human voices, the emotions of the ballads he writes can be understood even by people who don't prefer synth singing.
Video: Melody-lit sky by Sasanomaly (vb: Nightcord at 25:00)

Project Sekai: Colorful Stage! is a rhythm game by SEGA that has a music setlist of Vocaloid songs. Aside from adding already existing Vocaloid songs into the game, SEGA also commissions Vocaloid Producers to create original songs for the game's events. Aside from the 6 cryptonloids, the game's cast also features 20 original characters that are all tied to Hatsune Miku.
Video: Journey by DECO*27 (vb: Hatsune Miku and Project Sekai Unit Leaders)
